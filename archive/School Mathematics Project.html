How to make a Potato and Cheese Omelette

Ingredients

To make a Potato and Cheese Omelette for 2:

  • 1 Cup Onion
  • 1/2 Cup Green Peppers (chopped)
  • 1/2 Cup Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup milk
  • 2 small sized baby Potato (thinly sliced)
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 3/4 cup of grated Cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Preparation:

  1. Heat the pan and add 1 tablespoon of butter. Saute the sliced potato in a pan until it is crisp.
  2. Add 4 eggs to a bowl and the milk and whisk well. Add salt and chilli powder and mix well.
  3. In another pan, saute the chopped onions and green peppers. Add tomato and salt to taste.
  4. Pour the beaten eggs on top of it.
  5. Place fried potato slices on it and sprinkle some pepper powder.
  6. Add grated cheese on top and close it with a lid. Cook on low flame for 2 min.
  7. Once cooked transfer to a plate, cut into slices and eat.
