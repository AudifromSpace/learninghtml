Protagonists:

Tintin:

Tintin is a reporter, adventurer, traveler, and the protagonist of the popular comic book series The Adventures of Tintin.

Snowy

Snowy Is Tintin's pet wire fox terrier.He and Tintin went on adventures and solved them.

Captain Archibald Haddock

Captain Archibald Haddock is a retired merchant sailor and Tintin's Best Friend since the book The Crab With Golden Claws.

Professor Cuthbert Calculus

He is a professor and inventor first appearing in Red Rackham's Treasure offering to help Tintin and Captain Haddock on their treasure hunt to find the pirate Red Rackham's treasure hidden by Captain Haddock's anscestor Sir Francis Haddock by giving them his Sharkproof-Shark Submarine.

Thomson and Thompson

Thomson and Thompson also known as "The Thompson Twins," are a pair of inept Scotland Yard detectives first appearing in Cigars of the Pharaoh.They are identical and are only discernible by the shape o their moustaches.

Antagonists

Roberto Rastapopoulos

He is Tintin's archenemy, a ruthless business tycoon. Rastapopoulos is the owner of Cosmos Pictures, a studio house that is a front for many of his illegal activities. He goes by a number of pseudonyms, including Marquis di Gorgonzola in The Red Sea Sharks.
